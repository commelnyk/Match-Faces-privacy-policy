Match Faces – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

Match Faces (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All photos selected by the user and all camera images are processed locally on your device only. The App does not send images, facial data, or any other information to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

2. Image Processing

The App allows users to:

  • Load photos from their device
  • Use the front or rear camera to detect faces
  • Compare facial features

When a face is detected, the App calculates similarity values between facial features such as:

  • Eyes
  • Nose
  • Lips
  • Overall facial similarity

These calculations occur entirely on the user's device and are not saved after the comparison session.

The App may display visual overlays such as animated points on detected faces to illustrate feature detection and comparison.

3. Data Storage

The App does not store:

  • Photos
  • Facial data
  • Biometric identifiers
  • User accounts
  • Usage history

Any images used in the App remain on your device and are handled by the operating system.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking systems, or external services.

5. Entertainment Purpose and Disclaimer

Match Faces is created for entertainment purposes only.

Similarity results shown in the App are approximate and not scientifically accurate. The calculations should not be considered reliable measurements of identity, resemblance, or biometric verification.

The developers of the App:

  • do not guarantee the accuracy of similarity calculations
  • are not responsible for decisions made based on the results
  • provide the App “as is” without warranties or guarantees

By using the App, users acknowledge that all results are informational and provided for entertainment only.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Face Data Handling (Apple Requirements)

This section explicitly describes how the App handles face data in accordance with App Store Review Guidelines.

Collection of Face Data

The App does not collect face data. Facial information is processed only temporarily on the user’s device during active use of the App.

Use of Face Data

Face data is used solely to perform real-time face detection and similarity comparison between images selected by the user or captured by the camera. This processing is done exclusively on-device and is required for the core functionality of the App.

Storage and Retention of Face Data

The App does not store or retain face data. All face data is processed in memory and is immediately discarded after the comparison is completed or the session ends.

Sharing and Disclosure of Face Data

The App does not share, transmit, or disclose face data to any third parties, servers, or external services.

User Control

All images and face data remain under the user’s control on their device. The user may stop using the App at any time, which immediately stops all face data processing.
